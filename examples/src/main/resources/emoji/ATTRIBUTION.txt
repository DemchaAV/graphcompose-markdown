Twemoji
=======

The emoji PNGs in this directory (rocket, white_check_mark, tada, fire, star) are from
Twemoji — https://github.com/twitter/twemoji — used here to demonstrate emoji rendering.

Copyright 2020 Twitter, Inc and other contributors.
Graphics licensed under CC-BY 4.0: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Files are named by their Markdown shortcode (e.g. rocket.png for :rocket:). Add more by
dropping the corresponding Twemoji 72x72 PNG, renamed to <shortcode>.png, into this
folder.
